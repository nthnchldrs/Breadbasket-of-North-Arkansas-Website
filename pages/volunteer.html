Be the Change

Join the dedicated team of volunteers at The Breadbasket of North Arkansas and make a direct impact in the fight against hunger in our community. Whether you have a few hours a month or can commit more regularly, your time and talents can drive real change.

Volunteer Opportunities

Urgent Needs:

Food Sorting and Packing: Help us organize and prepare donations for distribution.

Delivery Assistance: Deliver food directly to those in need, especially crucial for our homebound or elderly community members.

Ongoing Volunteer Roles:

Community Outreach: Represent The Breadbasket at local events and spread awareness about our mission.

Administrative Support: Assist with data entry, answering phones, and other office tasks.

Educational Programs: Assist in teaching nutritional classes or workshops.

How to Get Involved

Step 1: Express Intrest

Fill out our online form below to tell us a bit about yourself and your interest in volunteering.

Step 2: Orientation

Attend a volunteer orientation session to learn more about The Breadbasket's mission, or community impact, and specific volunteer roles.'

Step 3: Start Volunteering

Based on your interest and our needs, we'll match you with the best volunteer opportunities. From there, you're ready to make a difference.

Why Volunteer?

Volunteering at The Breadbasket not only helps those in need but also enriches your own life. It’s a chance to:

  • Connect with your community.
  • Build new skills and gain valuable experience.
  • Be part of a supportive and caring team.
  • Make a tangible impact in the fight against hunger.

Ready to join us? Fill out the volunteer form today, and let’s start making a difference together.
