Make a Difference Today

Your generosity helps The Breadbasket of North Arkansas feed families, fuel hope, and foster community. Every donation, big or small, supports our mission to combat hunger and provide resources for individuals and families in need.

Ways to Donate:

Donate Funds

Online: Donate with your debit or credit card today!

By Mail: Send your check to the listed address, making it payable to "The Breadbasket of North Arkansas"

Matching Gifts: Double your impact! Check if your employer offers a matching gift program.

Donate Food

Drop Off: Bring non-perishable food items to our location at [address]. See our list of most needed items below.

Host a Food Drive: Rally your community to collect essential food items. Contact us for a starter kit and support.

Why Your Donation Matters

Your contribution supports our comprehensive efforts to address hunger in North Arkansas. It allows us to:

  • Purchase and distribute nutritious food.
  • Run programs that educate and empower our community.
  • Provide emergency food assistance to families in crisis.

What Can Be Donated

Most Needed Items:

  • Canned fruits and vegetables
  • Whole grain pasta and rice
  • Non-perishable protein sources (peanut butter, canned tuna)
  • Baby formula and diapers

Please Note: We cannot accept open packages, homemade items, or products beyond their expiration date.
