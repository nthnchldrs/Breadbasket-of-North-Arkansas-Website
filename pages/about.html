Our Mission

At The Breadbasket of North Arknasas, our mission is to fight hunger and nourish our community through dedicated service, education, and advocacy. We believe that access to nutritious food is a basic human right and are committed to ensuring athta no one in North Arkansas has to go to bed hungry.

Our Story

Founded in 2005 by a group of community members concerned about the rising levels of food insecurity in our region, The Breadbasket of North Arkansas has grown from a small pantry to a key organization in the fight against hunger. Through the years, our efforts have expanded to include food distribution, nutritional education programs, and advocacy for food security policies.

Our Services

The Breadbasket of North Arkansas is dedicated to providing a range of services aimed at combating hunger and supporting those in need in our community. Here’s how we’re making a difference:

  • Food Distribution: We offer direct food assistance to individuals and families in need through our pantry services, emergency food boxes, and mobile pantry outreach.
  • Nutritional Education: Our programs teach participants how to prepare healthy, affordable meals, emphasizing the importance of nutrition in overall well-being.
  • Volunteer Opportunities: We welcome individuals and groups to contribute their time and skills, whether through sorting donations, assisting with food distribution, or participating in educational outreach.
  • Advocacy and Awareness: We strive to raise awareness about hunger issues in North Arkansas and advocate for policies that address food insecurity at the local and state levels.
  • Community Partnerships: By collaborating with local schools, businesses, and other nonprofits, we extend the reach of our services and amplify our impact.
  • Support Services Referral: We connect individuals and families with additional resources and services beyond food assistance, including housing support, financial counseling, and healthcare resources.

Our comprehensive approach ensures that we not only address immediate food needs but also work towards long-term solutions for food insecurity in our community.
